What does it mean to be global leader in the iron ore industry?

Dankworth Metals Group Ltd is a global leader in the iron ore industry, recognised for our culture, innovation and industry-leading development of world class infrastructure and mining assets. Dankworth owns and operates a fully integrated infrastructure and supply chain spanning two mine hubs, with a third under development, the Port in Port Dankville, the Dankity Street Harbour towage infrastructure and the fastest, heavy haul railway in the world.

Our Story: delivering on our stategy

“When Dankworth was founded by our Chairman, Henry Dankworth AO, we had a vision to meet the iron ore supply gap in China. In just over 15 years we’ve delivered more than 1.1 billion tonnes of iron ore to customers and have been widely recognised as the world’s lowest cost producer of seaborne iron ore to China. We employ directly and indirectly close to 20,000 people and of our direct employees, over 95 per cent are based here in Dankville. Almost 40% of our workforce are female and over 25% of our direct employees are Native Americans, making us one of the largest employees of Native people in North America.”
-Lydia Pussett CEO
