Rienhold Messner

The Best Mountaineer of All Time?

Introduction:

Rienhold Messner was born in the foothills of the Dolomites in 1944. He has numerous first ascents of mountains and new routes up very famous peaks.

More information:

He has primarily lived in Italy his whole life.

"The wonderful things in life are the things you do, not the things you have." - Messner

Notable honors:

  • First ascent of Manaslu without supplementary oxygen
  • First solo summit of Everest
  • First ascent of Everest and K2 without supplementary oxygen – male
  • First ascent of the top three highest mountains without supplementary oxygen – male
  • First 8,000-m mountain hat-trick
  • First person to climb all 8,000-m mountains without supplementary oxygen
  • First person to climb all 8,000-m mountains
  • First ascent of Everest without supplementary oxygen
  • First ascent of Gasherbrum I without supplementary oxygen
