A Brief Bio of Céline Dion

Céline Dion began her career as a teenager singing in French in the 80s. She released her first album in 1981. In 1989, to perfect her English, she attended École Berlitz. Ms. Dion's first English-language album was released in 1990.

At the age of 12, she met her late husband and manager, René Angélil (age 38 at the time), in 1980 after her brother, Michel Dondalinger Dion, sent Mr. Angélil a recording of Céline. From 2003 to 2007, Dion held a three-year, five nights a week, 600-show contract at The Colosseum at Caesars Palace in Las Vegas. Her second Vegas residency launched in 2011 through 2019.
